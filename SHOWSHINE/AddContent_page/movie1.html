Dear Zindagi
2016 | 2h 31m | IMDb 7.4/10


Dear Zindagi is a Hindi-language coming-of-age drama released in 2016. The film is directed by Gauri Shinde and produced by Karan Johar and Gauri Khan. It stars Alia Bhatt and Shah Rukh Khan in the lead roles. The movie tells the story of Kaira (Alia Bhatt), a young woman struggling with anxiety and relationship problems. She meets Dr. Jehangir Khan (Shah Rukh Khan), a therapist, who helps her face her fears and anxieties and teaches her how to find happiness and meaning in her life. The film tackles issues such as mental health, self-discovery, and the importance of seeking help and support when dealing with emotional struggles. It received critical acclaim for its sensitive portrayal of mental health issues and Alia Bhatt`s performance.
