Kabir Singh
2019 | 2h 53m | IMDb 7.0/10


"Kabir Singh" is a Bollywood romantic drama movie that was released in 2019. The movie is a Hindi-language remake of the Telugu film "Arjun Reddy" which was released in 2017. The film features Shahid Kapoor and Kiara Advani in the main roles, and portrays the story of a talented yet hotheaded medical student who falls in love with a fellow student. However, when their relationship comes to an end, he loses control and falls into a self-destructive spiral. Despite receiving mixed reviews from critics, "Kabir Singh" was a commercial hit, grossing over 3.5 billion rupees at the box office.
